WORDLE

Summary

The "Wordle" remake was a personal project taken up by myself during my time on co-op. Utilizing time on my lunch break, I found enjoyment from working on the logic of this "Wordle" remake. The largest difficulty came from the logic challenges associated with the evaluation of the user's answer. There are many combinations of conditions that needed to be addressed. Some of the most challenging being (x representing some letter):

  • Multiple x's exist, only one x guessed
  • One x exists, multiple x's guessed
  • Multiple x's exist, one x guessed in position, one out of position
  • Multiple x's exist, multiple x's guessed out of postion

As you can see the biggest issues pertained to words that have multiple of the same letter. This posed a fun challenge that required more thinking than many standard in-class examples that are focused on basic programming principles. I also found the creation of multiple new aesthetic pieces to mend with the logic.
